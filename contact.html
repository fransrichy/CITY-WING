Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my shuttle service?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for standard services. For airport transfers, 24 hours is usually sufficient, but during peak seasons we suggest booking 3-5 days ahead to ensure availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 50% charge. No-shows are charged the full amount. For special packages and tours, please refer to the specific terms in your booking confirmation.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we provide child safety seats at no additional cost. Please specify your requirements (age and weight of child) when making your booking so we can ensure the appropriate seat is available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (Namibian Dollars), credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and bank transfers. For online bookings, we accept all major credit cards. Corporate accounts can be set up for regular clients with monthly billing.

Do your drivers speak English?

Yes, all our drivers are fluent in English. Many also speak Afrikaans, German, and local languages. If you have specific language requirements, please let us know when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

What areas in Namibia do you service?

We operate throughout Namibia with regular services in Windhoek, Swakopmund, Walvis Bay, and to major tourist destinations including Etosha National Park, Sossusvlei, and the Skeleton Coast. We can arrange transportation to any destination in Namibia - just let us know your requirements.
